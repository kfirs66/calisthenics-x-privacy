CalisthenicsX – Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

CalisthenicsX ("we", "us", or "our") operates the CalisthenicsX mobile application (the "App"). This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We store this in our database to identify you within the App.

We also collect content you create: workout logs, posts, videos, photos, comments, and personal records that you voluntarily submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your profile and content to other users
  • To provide personalized training programs and workout tracking
  • To send push notifications about activity on your posts (likes, comments, follows)
  • To improve the App and fix issues

3. Media Uploads

Videos and photos you post are uploaded to Cloudinary, a cloud media service, where they are stored and served. By posting media, you grant us a non-exclusive license to display it within the App.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, cloud messaging) – firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In – policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudinary (media storage) – cloudinary.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties except as required to operate the services listed above, or if required by law.

Content you post (videos, photos, workout logs, comments) is visible to other authenticated users of the App.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile → Settings → Delete Account screen. This permanently removes your account and all associated data from our systems.

Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the CalisthenicsX app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion

Your account and all data (posts, workout logs, comments) will be permanently deleted immediately.

7. Children's Privacy

CalisthenicsX is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure users can only access their own private data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@calisthenics-x.com
